69 Space Tech Startups to Watch (and Work for) in 2024

Space Tech is rapidly transforming the way we think about and interact with the world around us. Across Europe, Space Tech startups are pushing boundaries, fixing problems worth solving, and reshaping the contours of their industry.

In 2023, Space Tech startups have raised $850 Million, and as we get into 2024, we dive into our curated list of top Space Tech startups for the year. The list kicks off with companies like ZeroAvia, SkyNRG and Isar Aerospace but all startups are worth diving into.

To ensure objectivity, we update and sort this list dynamically every month using our Seedtable score and our database of 17,000 technology companies in Europe. Whether you're an investor scouting for the next big thing, an industry professional looking for inspiration, or simply curious about the latest in Space Tech, this list provides a comprehensive snapshot of the most promising players in the field.
